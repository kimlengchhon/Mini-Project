Our restaurant is a high-end Cambodian restaurant that prides itself on offering Living Cambodian Cuisine. Through dedicated research and promotion of Cambodian culinary traditions, we strive to preserve our nation's rich food heritage. Our dishes are inspired by the ancient Angkor period and highlight the unique flavors of Cambodia.
